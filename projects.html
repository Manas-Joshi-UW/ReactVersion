FitBot — Messenger Bot





With the sudden popularity of Chatbots, a team of 3 and I created this chatbot that acts as a personal gym assistant. I personally worked with the Google Places API to provide user's with GoodLife Gym Locations and their respective ratings. I also added a "Workout" feature where a user can add, modify, or delete a workout they may have learned. Stored each "workout" as an object to better help the user access their workouts based on muscle groups.

Languages:

JavaScript, Node.js

Brick Breaker





After I learned the basics of JavaScript, I decided to make this Brick Breaker game as I realized that it would present certain challenges to learn from. The biggest challenge in making this game was to account for all the possibilities of collisions with the bricks. For example, I realized that the animation was weird if the ball hit the brick from the top. As a result, I was able to learn about certain functions that helped me create an enjoyable experience.

Traffic Light Detection





When I joined the UW Robotics Team, I instantly started working on a green light detector that would tell our autonomous car when to begin the race. I developed an algorithm that works even when the car is far away and when the car is looking at the light from different angles. Through this experience, I started learning about MATLAB and image-processing techniques.

Languages:

MATLAB
